# Code of Conduct

## Pledge

Participation in this project should be harassment-free for everyone, regardless of experience level, age, gender, sexual orientation, disability, appearance, ethnicity, religion, or nationality.

## Standards

Encouraged: respectful disagreement, constructive feedback given and received gracefully, focus on what's best for the project, empathy toward other contributors.

Not tolerated: insults, personal or political attacks, harassment (public or private), publishing others' private information without consent, sexualized language or imagery.

## Scope

Applies to all project spaces (issues, pull requests, discussions) and to anyone officially representing the project elsewhere.

## Enforcement

Report violations to [nils.dontot.pro@gmail.com](mailto:nils.dontot.pro@gmail.com). Reports are reviewed promptly; reporter privacy is respected.

Consequences scale with severity: a private warning for a first minor incident, up to a permanent ban for sustained harassment or serious violations. The maintainer decides case by case.
